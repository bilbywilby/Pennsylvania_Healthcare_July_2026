Critical Distinction Notes
Veterans' Access Preservation: VA CCN participants maintain uninterrupted in-network status with LVHN facilities regardless of commercial contract separation. This exemption derives from separate federal-state procurement agreement architecture isolating veteran healthcare procurement from private commercial negotiations.

Balance Billing Protection Limitations: Members accessing LVHN services post-termination outside continuity provisions face standard out-of-network reimbursement schedules. Emergency care retains baseline protection under NCQA accreditation requirements; non-emergent services subject to full charge master rates absent network negotiation.
